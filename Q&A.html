What is EWC all about?

EWC (Empowering Women Through Code) is a girl's coding club at Valley View High School that allows us to creates a safe space for girls from all backgrounds who are interested in code.

Do I have to have any experience to join?

No, not at all! We specifically made this club for young women who are complete beginners. We want to remove the stigma that you have to be an anti-social, neckbeard, weird scrawny-looking person to become a programmer. Even out officers come from all different levels of coding experience.
